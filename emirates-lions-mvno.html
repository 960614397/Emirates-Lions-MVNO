SECTION 01

What is an MVNO?

A Mobile Virtual Network Operator (MVNO) is a brand that sells mobile services without owning the underlying radio network. It rides on a host MNO's coverage and competes on brand, customer experience, and the value layered on top of the SIM.

MNO · Mobile Network Operator

Owns the towers, spectrum and core network. Provides wholesale capacity.

MVNE · Mobile Virtual Network Enabler

Operates the BSS, OCS, provisioning and day-to-day commercial running of the MVNO. This is us.

That's us

MVNO · Mobile Virtual Network Operator

Owns the brand, the customer relationship and the go-to-market. The face of the business.

Why brands launch MVNOs

01

Non-comparative proposition

Bundle connectivity into your existing brand promise — a price that can't be apples-to-apples compared with an MNO.

02

Greater customer entanglement

Mobile services touch every day of your customer's life. Loyalty becomes structural, not promotional.

03

First-party behavioural data

Passively collected usage data feeds personalisation, retention and new revenue lines.

04

New high-margin revenue

Layered VAS — fintech, security, content, insurance — unlock margins that pure connectivity cannot.

SECTION 03

How we build MVNOs

Our operating model abstracts the technical and operational complexity of running an MVNO so you can focus on brand, proposition and growth. Five deliverables, sequenced — from commercial viability to launch.

The three-layer operating model

Host MNO

Provides network infrastructure, wholesale billing, SIM procurement, voucher distribution.

MVNE — DSG

Manages technical operations, subscriber management, billing & invoicing, inbound customer care, business support.

MVNO — You

Owns brand, marketing, sales, distribution, credit relationship and loyalty.

Five deliverables — sequenced

Every engagement starts with commercial viability and ends with an MVP ready to take to market.

01

03

Sales, distribution, customer care and full subscriber lifecycle management — from acquisition through retention to win-back.
